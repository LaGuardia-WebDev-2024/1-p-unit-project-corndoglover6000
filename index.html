Important fact: BISON ARE NOT BUFFALO
- but it is totally okay to call them that!
Buffalo has turned into a colloquial term for american bison! The most important thing is to know the difference.

Here we see a Plains Bison on the left, and a true Buffalo on the right. Pretty different, huh?

Bison Fun Facts!!

  • The current population of Plains Bison is approximately 45,000
  • They can weigh 700-2,000 pounds
  • They can grow to be 7-12 ft long
  • Their scientific name is Bison Bison Bison
  • Plains Bison are one of two subspecies of American Bison, the other being Woods Bison
  • Yellowstone National Park is the only place a Bison population has continuously lived since colonization
  • The American Bison is the national mammal of the United States

Native American Plains Tribes uses for Bison

Many Native American tribes have strong connections with the American Bison. Historically they have used all parts of the Bison for a variety of reasons
